Fluxuation

Watching Hannah Montanah as a kid, I was facinated.

The concept that someone "normal" could secretely be a superstar made me feel like I could be anything; like the people around me could secretely be anything- especially someone wonderful, admirable, valuable.

It didn't matter how people thought of me at school or if it didn't seem like I was very special. I knew looks could decieve or shroud the true nature of things. That thought gave me a lot of hope as a little weirdo who grew up with little and encouraged me to treat everyone with respect.
